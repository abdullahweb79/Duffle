Born in Sialkot. Trusted by the world. Dufflecraft Co. brings together Pakistan's finest leather tradition and the demands of a global marketplace — one handcrafted bag at a time.

Dufflecraft Co. is a premium leather goods manufacturer and exporter based in Sialkot, Pakistan — the city that has defined the world's leather craftsmanship for over a century. We design, manufacture, and export genuine leather bags and accessories to retailers, wholesalers, and brands across Germany, the United Kingdom, the United States, Europe, and the Middle East.

We are more than a supplier. We are guardians of a craft passed down through generations — bringing irreplaceable human skill to every product that leaves our workshop.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering wholesale leather goods from DuffleCraftCo.

Where is DuffleCraftCo located and where do you export?

DuffleCraftCo is based in Sialkot, Punjab, Pakistan — the world's leather manufacturing capital. We export premium leather bags and accessories to 15+ countries including USA, Germany, UK, France, Netherlands, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Australia, and Canada.

Do you offer wholesale and bulk leather bag orders?

Yes. We specialize in wholesale and bulk leather bag orders for retailers, distributors, and brands worldwide. We supply leather duffle bags, business bags, crossbody bags, sports bags, tactical bags, and accessories in large quantities with competitive pricing.

Do you offer OEM and private label leather goods manufacturing?

Yes. DuffleCraftCo offers complete OEM and private label manufacturing. You can fully customize products with your brand logo, custom designs, hardware finishes, colors, and packaging. We handle the full production process from design to delivery.

What type of leather do you use in your products?

We use only full-grain and top-grain genuine leather — the highest quality grades available. Our leather is sourced and selected specifically for durability, texture, and natural character that develops beautifully with age and use.
